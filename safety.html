// online safety · for parents

Safe by design.

Your kid is online, with people you haven't met, learning something you may not. We take that seriously. Here's exactly how it works — in plain terms, no fine print.

  • ✓Live, never one-on-one. Every class is a small group with an instructor leading it start to finish. Your kid is never alone with someone.
  • ✓Parents are always welcome. Sit in on any class, any week — you're always welcome in the room, watching exactly how it runs.
  • ✓Everything runs in the browser. Nothing to download or install. No new app living on your kid's device.
  • ✓We talk to parents, not around them. Anything outside of class time comes to you. We don't message students privately or ask them for personal details.
  • ✓No account, nothing to install. Applying takes about three minutes and asks only for what we need to reach you — and a founder reads every one before anyone joins.
  • ✓Small cohorts, on purpose. Twelve seats means we genuinely know every student in the room — no one slips through.

Code Share Academy is a real, structured program — not a side project — and you'll always reach a founder directly, not a bot. Still have a question about safety? Text or call (425) 677-5903, or email and we'll answer it ourselves.
